About Me:


नमस्कार! I am Achyut Mani Tripathi (अच्युत मणि त्रिपाठी), originally from Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, India. I am an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science & Engineering Department at the Indian Institute of Technology Dharwad (IIT-Dh) in Karnataka, India. I earned my Ph.D. in 2021 from the Computer Science & Engineering Department at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IIT-Guwahati) in Assam, India.
My Research Interests Include:
Deep Learning for Environmental Sound Classification, Lightweight Large Acoustic Models, Adversarial Attacks, Knowledge Distillation, Multimodal Learning, Audio-Visual Event Localization (AVEL), Audio-Visual Segmentation (AVS), Audio-Visual Scene Classification (AVSC), Audio-Visual Question Answering (AVQA), Audio Visual Source Localization(AVSL), Audio-Visual Segmentation (AVS), Continual Learning for Audio Classification, Federated Acoustic Meta-Learning and Predictive Maintenance.
